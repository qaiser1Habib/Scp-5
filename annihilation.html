SCP: ANNIHILATION

Follow SCP Agent “D”, the lone survivor of an anomalous event that has engulfed a Foundation installation deep beneath the tourist village of Land’s End.

Explore a Sunken Metropolis

Navigate the environments of a secret SCP Foundation site ravaged by an cataclysmic event. Secure, contain, and protect the secrets of the city from the breached anomalies that roam the deconstructed city.

Uncover the city’s mysteries through, an amnesiac

Melancholic Character

Agent D is aided by Agent J ( later revealed to be SCP-5094 ) who communicates via an in-suit mobile device and communication terminals scattered around The City. Agent J assists Agent D in tracking the containment breaches across the City. Concurrently Agent D uncovers pertinent information about the missing Foundation personnel, and begins to unravel the mystery of the Event that engulfed the City.

Contain the Escaped SCPs!

As Agent D explores the environment she will encounter several escaped anomalies roaming the uninhabited structures. The player will make allegiances with some, while “containing” others. Compelled to explore the depths of The City, Agent “D” descends into its labyrinthine architecture learning The City’s nature as well as her own.
