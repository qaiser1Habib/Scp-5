We stand apart from the natural order.

Our game art promotes the weird and horrific. Our stories border on “the insane, with long intervals of horrible sanity”. We narrate the futility of righteousness, the hypocrisy of political authority, and the purity of primordial fear. We exploit adversarial, vindictive, and morose themes to paint our worlds in the colours of gothic beauty.

Our heroes are not cliches – they are neither noble nor virtuous. They are troubled, dark souls with relentlessly violent lives and tragic ends.

Our workplace culture is inclusive of those who cherish gothic horror, weird tales, and Eldritch cosmicism.
