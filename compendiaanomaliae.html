SCP: COMPENDIA ANOMALIAE

A collection of paranormal, supernatural and other inexplicable phenomena from the SCP: Foundation.

Our agents have commenced producing high quality renderings of at-large and contained SCPs. Angry Octopus is investigating available art production pipelines technologies to aid in the expansion of the Compendia, including AI Art engines. We constantly explore and interogate emerging technologies, artists and modalities to publish our investigations, to effect plausible deniability.
